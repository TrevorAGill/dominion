Dominion

This is a game of building a deck of cards. The deck is your Dominion. It contains your resources, victory points, and the things you can do. It starts out as a small sad collection of Estates and Coppers, but you hope by the end of the game it will be brimming with Gold, Provinces, and the inhabitants and structures of your castle and kingdom. The player with the most victory points in his deck at game end (when all Provinces have been purchased) wins the game.

  • The game is turn-based
  • Each turn, a player draws a hand of 5 cards from their deck
  • The first phase of your turn is the Action phase--if you have an action card in your hand you can play it to get more money, actions, or buys
  • The second phase of your turn is the Buy phase--you can use the money in your hand to buy a card from the store. The purchased card will go into your discard pile
  • When you end your turn, all cards from your hand are moved into the discard pile and the next player draws their hand
  • The game ends when all Province cards have been purchased (there are 12 in a deck)--at that point, players add up their total number of victory points--whoever has the most wins the game!
